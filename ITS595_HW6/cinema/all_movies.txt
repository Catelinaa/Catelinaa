"Sound of Metal," "CODA," and "Sweet Nothing in My Ear" are films that portray the experiences of Deaf individuals in society.
"Sound of Metal" focuses on a drummer who loses his hearing and explores the challenges of adjusting to a Deaf world.
"CODA" is about a hearing child in a Deaf family who faces the decision of pursuing her dream while balancing family duties.
"Sweet Nothing in My Ear" tells the story of a Deaf couple facing the decision of whether to have their son undergo a cochlear implant surgery.
